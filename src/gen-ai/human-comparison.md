# Human Comparison
Many people have taken to using Gen AI as a metaphor for people, so that they can handle otherwise toxic discussions in plain sight.

The reason that the metaphor works so well is because one has been borne out of the other.

## Agentic Experiences
Agentic experiences provide reliable execution of predefined tasks. These are more human equivalents of APTs ([Automatically Programmed Tools](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/APT_(programming_language))).
